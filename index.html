About RYFI

Rivers Youth Future Initiative is a youth-centered organisation founded in 2022. We believe that every young person in the Niger Delta deserves access to quality education, digital skills, and a platform to voice their ideas.

We partner with local schools, tech hubs, and international donors to deliver impactful programs in STEAM, entrepreneurship, and environmental stewardship.

2,400+ youth reached
18 partner organisations
85% employment/up-skilling rate

Our mission

“To unlock the potential of Rivers youth through inclusive education, mentorship, and innovation, fostering a generation of leaders who drive sustainable development.”
